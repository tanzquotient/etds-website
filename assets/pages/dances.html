Dances

The dances that you have to dance differ per class in which you are placed. Furthermore, this may mean you get additional dances in later rounds. You can find the information regarding classes on the classes page.

Breitensport Standard

Breitensport qualification: SW, TQ, QS

Finals:

  • Enthusiasts + Amateurs: SW, TQ, QS
  • Professionals + Masters: also WW in later rounds
  • Champions + Legends: also WW, SF in later rounds

Breitensport Latin

Breitensport qualification: CC, RU, JI

Finals:

  • Enthusiasts + Amateurs: CC, RU, JI
  • Professionals + Masters: also SA in later rounds
  • Champions + Legends: also SA, PD in later rounds

ClOpen

Be prepared to dance any of the five dances at any point of the competition.

Legend

  • Slow Waltz - SW
  • Tango - TG
  • Viennese Waltz - VW
  • Slow Foxtrot - SF
  • Quickstep - QS
  • SA - Samba
  • CC - Cha Cha Cha
  • RU - Rumba
  • PD - Paso Doble
  • JI - Jive
