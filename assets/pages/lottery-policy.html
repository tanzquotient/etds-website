Lottery Policy

The ETDS ZÃ¼rich will make use of the lottery system as implemented by the xTDS system.

The lottery system has the purpose of reducing stress in the registration period while ensuring a balance between leads and follows. Furthermore, it guarantees that any team that signs up gets the possibility to represent themselves during the ETDS.

For more information regarding the registration procedure, see the registration policy.

Conditions

In order to fairly select the participants for the ETDS, a lottery system will be used. The lottery system randomly drafts people from the registration list that are selected for participation. In order to give all registrants a fair chance of participating, the lottery system takes into account the following conditions.
  • Couples registered together will not be split up.
  • Team captains are guaranteed to participate, the number of team captains depends on the size of the team.
  • Participants are selected to maintain leader/follower balance in all classes.
  • Registrants who have not joined an ETDS before have a slight preference in the lottery system.
  • Registrants who registered in the previous ETDS but were not drafted have a very slight preference in the lottery system. For this, signup with the same email address is crucial.

Rounds

One main lottery rounds will be held, selecting the majority of the participants. Further selections will be made based on cancellations and evening out the lead/follow counts.

Partners

If a person who registered with a partner gets drafted, that partner will also be selected for participation automatically. Therefore, a dancing couple that registers together will always be selected together.

If a person who registered without a partner gets drafted, the lottery system will select an additional person who registered without a partner. This additional person will be selected based on what is needed to maintain a good leader/follower balance. This will make sure that as many participants as possible can find a partner during blind dating.

Selection Procedure

At least 90% of the participants will be selected using the lottery system outlined above. The remaining spots are reserved for special purposes including but not limited to: the organization, tournament office, super-volunteers, first-aid volunteers, other specific volunteer functions, last-minute balancing of leader/follower distribution. These spots are appointed by the organization.
