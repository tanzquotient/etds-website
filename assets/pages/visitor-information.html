Visitor information

Did not get a place as a participant? You can still join as a visitor!

Day Visitors

If some of your friends or relatives are interested in coming to watch the tournament, they can come as day visitors. Spectators can enter the tournament hall from 8:00 in the morning and are expected to leave the tournament hall until 30 minutes after the last final round of the tournament.

Evening Visitors

You are not a normal participant, but want to attend the evening partys? No problem! There will be tickets available as an evening visitor.

Tickets

Tickets for day and evening visitors will be available closer to the event. Please check back later for more information.
Because of space limitations the number of tickets will be limited.
