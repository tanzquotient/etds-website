Participant help

So... You cannot see the forest through the trees eh? (Pun intended) You can find all the basic information you need to know before and during the weekend right here on this page. We'll assume you have been to an ETDS (or NTDS) before. If not, check out the newcomers page.

For all questions, make sure you check with your team captain first. If they don't know, ask them to ask us. That way, we can keep all information flowing through the correct channels. If they don't respond, however, and you're panicking, then of course you can send us an email.

Before the ETDS

How do I register?
Registration goes through the team captains. If you are on this website, you will likely know who your team captain is. Payment also goes through your team captain or your association. If you don't know or don't have one, please send us an email.
My activation mail is not arriving!
First try by going to https://whitsun.xtds.dance/activate/new/example@example.com. Where you replace example@example.com by your own email. If this does not work, and you have checked your spam, please send us an email and we'll look into it!
Is there a packing list?
We have a packing list! You can find it here!
Can I park at the venue?
Yes, there are parking spaces available, but it is limited.

At the ETDS - General

Is there mandatory volunteering?
That is correct. Every participant is expected to help out to make the ETDS happen. Only in this way can we keep the costs low. Every team is assigned a number of OHHs (Obligatory Helping Hours). Division of these goes through the team captain.

Want to help out more? Great! You can enter this in the portal after logging in. If this doesn't work, or you don't know what to do, contact your team captain. Helping extra gives your team points to win the prize for the best overall team! For more information, see the volunteering page.
Will the sleeping halls stay open during the day/have to be cleared?
Good news, the sleeping halls do not have to be cleared during the day. You can just keep all your stuff in place.
Can I charge my phone at the venue?
There will not be a designated phone charging station. This means you have to bring your own extension cords for in the sleeping halls.
What does the team presentation entail?
Generally in an ETDS, teams are just called by city and the entire team walks on the floor.
Can my friends/family come and watch?
They are very welcome to come and watch you for the competitions. Note however that the number of tickets available is limited. For more information see the visitor information page.

Make sure to tell them the timetable, so they know when to come.

At the ETDS - Dancing

Is there a preliminary schedule?
Yes! You can find it in the schedule on the front page. It is still subject to change. A live timetable will be available during the competition.
Are there clothing restrictions?
Yes! They are generally the same as any ETDS. Check all restrictions on the dress code page!
Which dances do I have to dance?
The dances that you have to dance differ per class in which you are placed. Furthermore, this may mean you get additional dances in later rounds. You can find this information here.
