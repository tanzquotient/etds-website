Adjudicator help

So... You are planning to judge at the ETDS, or have already been asked? This is the page for you.

If anything is still unclear, please contact your team captain or us.

How do I register to be a judge?
Participating judges can register via the team captains through the regular system.

External judges are handled personally. If you haven't been contacted and would like to judge, feel free to contact us.

In both cases registration does not guarantee being chosen to adjudicate.
When will I know if what and I will have to judge?
External adjudicators will know at least 1 month in advance if they are chosen to adjudicate. Participating adjudicators will hear this at least 2 weeks in advance.

The first planning of adjudicators is expected to be posted here 2 weeks in advance. Of course, changes to the planning may be posted at any time.
What system do we use?
We use a digital adjudication system attached to the xTDS portal. This is done through a simple web application, accessible through any smartphone or tablet. You will receive your login information prior to the weekend so you can test it.
When should I be there?
All adjudicators without prior experience with the xTDS system are expected at the tournament hall at 08:00. All adjudicators with prior experience are expected at 08:30 for the initial briefing and information about the latest updates in the system.

Adjudicators that are not on time (or ask questions which would have been answered if they had shown up to the earlier briefing) will be required to do a forfeit. The forfeit may include doing 10 pushups in the middle of the dance floor with everyone watching, or anything else deemed fit by the organizing committee.

In other words, just don't be late!
What do I need to bring?
Of course you will need your expertise, a good mood, and your best outfit. Furthermore, you will need a charged smartphone or tablet. If you are unable to bring one or do not own one, please let us know and we will arrange one for you.
Are food and drinks provided?
Food and drinks are provided. Make sure you tell us or your team captain any dietary restrictions.
Can I sleep at the venue?
If you are an external adjudicator and would like to sleep at the venue, please contact us to see what we can do for you.
