Food & Drinks

It is very important that you get your nutrients and your fluids during the tournament.
Food is included for all participants. Food is NOT included for Day and Evening Visitors.

Food

Included meals

The food that will be provided is:
  • Breakfast, brunch and dinner on Saturday
  • Breakfast, brunch and dinner on Sunday
  • Breakfast on Monday

Brunch

Breakfast and lunch will be combined into a brunch. Food wil be served continuously from 07:30 until about 16:00. It will be a normal continental breakfast and self-service sandwich ingredients.

Dinner

A hot meal will be served on Saturday and Sunday evenings.


There will be vegetarian and vegan options. If you have other requirements, please let us know!


Preliminary menu:

  • Saturday: Mediterranean Pasta (vegan)
  • Sunday: Vegetable Risotto (vegetarian, vegan on request)

Plates & Cutlery

Please bring your own plate, cutlery, and cup.

You need these for breakfast/brunch and coffee/tea throughout the day. For dinner, our caterer will provide plates and cutlery.

Drinks

Coffee & Tea

Coffee and tea will be available all day (and night!) until we run out.

Bar

There will be NO bar. Tap water is freely available and of high quality.

I am still hungry/thirsty

You are allowed to bring your own food and drinks into the venue.


In particular, make sure to bring yourself some snacks/dinner for Friday evening.


There is a Coop supermarket within walking distance (~15 min). It is open Fri/Sat/Mon from 8:00-20:00, but closed on Sunday. There is also a mini-supermarket in a nearby petrol station (open 6:00-23:00 every day).
