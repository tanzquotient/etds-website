Privacy Policy

By registering for the European Tournament for Dancing Students, ETDS, as a participant, team captain, adjudicator, visitor or volunteer, you agree to the conditions in this privacy statement.

Who are we?

We are the organization of the European Tournament for Dancing Students (ETDS). The ETDS is a ballroom dancing tournament organized twice a year. Participation is only possible as part of a team. These teams will be provided by several student dancing associations in Europe in the name of their originated city. Every ETDS is organized by a different committee from one of the several participating cities. The ETDS of June 2025 will be organized by a committee of Team Zürich with assistance from commited individuals.

What kind of personal information do we collect?

We will collect the following personal information of every participant:
  • Name
  • Email address
  • Whether you are a student or not
  • Age
  • Dietary restrictions
  • Information related to your participation and accommodation

Who has access to your information?

Your data will not be shared with third parties. The following people will have access to your personal information:

The organization of the ETDS

Access to your personal information will be provided to a small amount of people that need the information to organize the tournament.

Your team captain

Your own team captain has access to all information shared by you, as he/she is the one registering you for the tournament.

Where will my information be saved?

Information of the registration will be saved on the servers of hosting provider TransIP BV in The Netherlands. The hosting provider does not have access to your data.

Changing my information

You can ask your team captain to change any incorrect information.

How long will my information be saved?

Your information will be saved at least until the end of the tournament. After that your name and results we be shared at the website www.etds.eu. Your name can be removed per request. After the tournament and before the start of the next tournament, your information can be used anonymously for statistical analysis, or be removed.

Can all my information be deleted?

Organizers may decide to give participants who are participating for the first time and advantage in the lottery system or offer them other advantages. If you have attended the tournament, your name and the fact that you participated will not be disconnected from your profile. All other information will be deleted as explained in the section above.  

Online published content

The tournament results (including participant names) may be published online. Photos and videos taken during the ETDS may be placed online and distributed via social media. Please contact us if you would like to be removed/excluded from this.
