In case of emergency

What to do

In case of emergency, the most important steps are:

  • Let your team captain or the info desk know
  • Let the organizing team know (ideally through the team captain)
  • For medical help, find first aid at the information desk. For medical emergencies call 144.
  • In case of fire or other emergencies, call 117 (police) or 118 (firefighters).
  • Make sure to relay the location of the event: Tägi Wettingen, Tägerhardstrasse 122, 5430 Wettingen.

Emergency numbers in Switzerland

112 always works, even without a SIM card. If you dial 112, you will be connected with the police emergency control centre. They can forward you as appropriate.

Of course, you can directly use the appropriate local number:

  • Police: 117
  • Fire service: 118
  • Ambulance: 144
  • Poison control: 145 (after calling 144)
