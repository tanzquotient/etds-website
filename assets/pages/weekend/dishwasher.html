Dishwasher manual

How to use

The dishwasher is located in the sleeping hall building, on the ground floor. The entry is through the triangular foyer.

There dishwasher is open Saturday and Sunday from 8:00 to 18:30.

There is no schedule. You can just drop by, put your stuff in, wait for it to finish, and take it out again. Ideally, you combine this within your team.

For dinner, plates and cutlery are provided by the caterer. You will only need to clean your dishes for the brunch.


See also the machine manual.

Things to consider

  • Make sure that your dishes are dishwasher proof.
  • Remove any leftover food from your plates by putting that in your trash bag attached at the end of your table.
  • Running the dishwasher takes only +/- 3 minutes. Make sure the responsible person stays present during the run of the dishwasher.
