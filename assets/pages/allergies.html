Allergies

While we have tried to accommodate everyone, these are the allergies for which special meals or substitutions have been arranged:

Saturday dinner

On Saturday, the main requests were for the food to be vegetarian or vegan. Since the meal will be vegan, those people are covered. The few exceptions have been mailed.

Other meals

For all other meals, many wishes, allergies, and intolerances have been taken into consideration. For these allergies, either:
  • The food does not contain it,
  • Or an alternative has been arranged.
