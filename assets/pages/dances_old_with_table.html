Dances

The dances that you have to dance differ per class in which you are placed. Furthermore, this may mean you get additional dances in later rounds. You can find the information regarding classes on the classes page.

For the first and second qualification rounds, only 3 dances are danced:
SW, TG, QS for Ballroom and CC, RB, JI for Latin.
