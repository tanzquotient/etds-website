General information

I did not receive my login information!
Oh no! We know that some email providers block the portal's emails. Please contact us directly!
Where do I login?
Just click the login button at the top-right of the page, or in the menu if you're on mobile. If you really can't find it, the address is whitsun.xtds.dance
What questions should you ask your team members before registration?
  • Name and surname
  • E-mail address
  • Student or non-student
  • Sleeping hall spot or not
  • First time ETDS or not
  • 18+ at time of tournament
  • Diet/Allergies
  • Dancing:
    • Role
    • Partner for ETDS (if you want to be selected for the lottery together)
  • Do you want to volunteer? (on top of your 1 obligatory helping hour)
  • Do you want to/can you volunteer as first aid?
  • Do you have an Emergency Response (BHV) license?
  • Do you want to/can you adjudicate?
Several of my team members were not selected last ETDS. Will they have a higher chance to get in now?
We have chosen to give a small preference to participants who were previously not selected for the ETDS. This does not mean, however, that they will be selected by default. For the participant to be eligible it is important that they sign up with the same email address as with which they signed up for the previous ETDS.
TC Checklist (and what you should tell your participants)
  • Before they register:
    • Send your members an awesome mail to make them even more enthusiastic
    • Where to look for information
    • Who to contact for what information (mostly you)
    • How and before when to register, including the necessary information
    • Make sure you check all class and blind date regulations for this person
  • After a lottery round:
    • Inform them that they have or have not been selected
    • Check if they still want to go and if so, confirm them in the system
    • How and when to pay
      • Also make sure you actually get the money transferred to us, the organization
  • Before the ETDS:
    • Think about a theme outfit (and actually have them get one)
    • Assign all OHHs (Obligatory Helping Hours)
    • Get them to maybe volunteer a bit extra
    • Coordinate your travels as a team
    • Make sure everyone knows what to take (see packing list)
    • In general make sure your teamies have all the necessary information to enjoy the ETDS experience to the fullest!
  • In case of cancellation:
    • Say that it may take until after the ETDS for the refund to be processed
    • In case of a late cancellation or empty waiting list, make sure the participant knows they may not get a (full) refund
    • Always make sure to cancel the registration in the portal
